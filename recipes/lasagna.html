Lasagna

Description

This recipe looks disgusting, but it's actually amazing. One of those rare situations where sewer-like colouring and random concocting of ingredients churns out something perfectly balanced.

Also, don't forget to bring a towel

Ingedients

  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato Souce
  • Basil
  • Cheese
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Saute the onions
  2. Do the same with the beef
  3. Add Tomato Sauce
  4. Simmer
  5. Add sauce to baking tin, cover with sheets, add sauce, cover with sheets, repeat, put in the oven
